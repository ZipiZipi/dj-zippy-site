About DJ Zippy

The Professional Choice for Unforgettable Events

DJ Zippy is a dynamic House and Techno DJ known for expertly crafted sets that build energy and keep dance floors engaged. With a deep understanding of crowd dynamics, he delivers a reliable and professional performance every time, making him the trusted choice for promoters seeking a seamless event.

With a resume that includes performances at major events like Exit Festival and headlining packed nights at renowned venues such as KC LAB and Krivi Put, DJ Zippy has proven his ability to perform in high-pressure environments. His focus on technical precision and unique track selection sets him apart as a dedicated artist and a dependable professional.
