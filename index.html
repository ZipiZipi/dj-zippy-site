THE ARTIST

Not a Producer.
A Selector.

In an era dominated by production credits, the art of selection is the true therapy. DJ Zippy specializes in reading the room, bridging the gap between the DJ booth and the dancefloor.

His brand, House Music Therapy, is built on the belief that the right groove can heal. While his foundation is strictly House, he adapts to the energy of the night, shifting into Techno when the atmosphere demands darker, driving rhythms.

Reliability is key. Through his collaboration with Go2 Travel, DJ Zippy has proven his adaptability, handling diverse crowds and non-EDM requirements with professional precision, making him a safe and exciting bet for any promoter.
